    Fire Alarm Technician

    VESDA Install at Frito Lay distribution plant
    My career for the past two years has been inspecting, servicing, and installing commercial fire alarms. Pictured above is about the biggest project I've had the pleasure of doing, it's a VESDA system which essentially samples air from those orange tubes over a protected space and can detect extremely small amounts of obscuration. Being a fire alarm technician is alot like being an electrician, the main difference being what I did was highly specialized and generally harder to get into. Over two years I've gathered many different certifications and licenses such as, NICET II, F.A.L. for Texas, Denver journeyman, and EST IO programmers cert. and many more although those are the important ones. Although I loved what I was doing my real passion has always been computers and everything they entail. Most recently I taking college classes in my free time but realized I needed a much more direct approach. So rather than going to school for 4+ years I decided to quit my job and join a coding bootcamp!
